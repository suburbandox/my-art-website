Mobius

First I created a Mobius strip out of paper, by cutting it, twisting it in half, and linking the two edges together with tape.  This creates a 3D shape in which both sides are continuous.  I think that this is an interesting form.
For the Mobius project, I used Autodesk AutoCAD to prove points. From this assignment, I learned how to use AutoCAD, and I used basic geometry principals to make new shapes.  I proved where midpoints are and connected sequential midpoints to create a line. I was inspired by August Ferdinand Mobius, who was related to Martin Luther, to succeed in this assignment.
